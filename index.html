Rock Paper Scissors

Instructions

Each round, you choose whether you will play rock, paper, or scissors. The computer will also make a randomly selected move. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock.
You will play until either you or the computer reaches the number of points you specify. One point is given to the winner of each round, but no points are awarded for ties.
Good luck!
